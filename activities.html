5 fun things to do in Stuisbaai and surrounds

Visit the Southern Tip of Africa

Only 7.3km from Southern Comfort Accommodation, L'Agulhas is known for being the southernmost town in Africa where the Indian and Atlantic oceans meet. Take your picture at the Southernmost tip of Africa and climb to the top of the local lighthouse.

Lounge on the Beach

Spend your days lounging on the longest uninterrupted white beach in the Southern Hemisphere, or the quaint little beach in the bustling fishing harbour.

Try catching a fish

From deep sea fishing charters to fishing off the harbour wall, the Southernmost Tip of Africa boasts a few ideal spots where you can wet your lines and try to catch the big ones.

See the famous Stingrays

One of the popular tourist attractions in Struisbaai is the large stringrays in the Struisbaai Harbour.
